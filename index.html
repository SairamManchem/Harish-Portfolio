About Me

I am a water resource engineer with over 7.5 years' experience in the field of storm water drainage projects majorly in Highway/Railway projects, with key tasks including storm-water drainage network analysis, culvert design, diversion channel, discharge assessment and various drainage proposals for protections works, underpasses and car parking layouts.

I also involved in the assignments related to infiltration/soakaways design, roadside hydrology, culvert design/optioneering, inlet spacing calculations & hydroplaning calculations. Acquired proficient knowledge in handling Civil 3D, Micro Drainage, SewerGEMS, HEC-HMS, HEC-RAS, HY-8, ArcGIS, Flow Master, and Flood modeler.

Currently based in Galway, Ireland, I bring specialized expertise in water management and drainage systems to infrastructure projects, focusing on sustainable solutions and climate change adaptation.
